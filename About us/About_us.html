About us

An election is a formal group decision-making process by which a population chooses an individual to hold public office. ... To elect means "to select or make a decision". Our website provides a modern and anonymous way to vote and elect leaders.

Voting process

  • Nomination of all the members of students council will be held as per the rules prescribed by the University and on the date announced by it.
  • The Student's council shall consist of the following :
    • Two students(male and female) from the each class,who has shown academic merit at the examination held in the preceding year and who is engaged in the full time studies int the college, elected by the students
  • The student members of the student council shall elect from amongst themselves,the secretary of their council and his or her name will be sent to the Students Council of college
